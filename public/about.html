Our History

Journey Since 2006

Gopalkrishna Vidhyaprasarak Saunstha was established in 2006 with the aim of providing quality education with strong moral values and discipline.

The institution began with pre-primary and primary sections and gradually expanded to middle and secondary education from the academic year 2020-21.

Today, GVS School stands as a trusted educational institution in Sankhali, focusing on holistic student development.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To nurture responsible, confident and knowledgeable citizens who contribute positively to society and nation.

Our Mission

To provide quality education through innovative teaching, moral values, discipline and holistic development in accordance with NEP 2020.

President's Message
